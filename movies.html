Iden's Favourite Movies (In no particular order)

Movie Title Thoughts IMDB
Alien (1979)
 A classic sci-fi horror with fantastic art design, an intense atmosphere, and a badass female lead. Spawned a long-running franchise that, in my opinion, has never lived up to the original. 8.4/10
The Dark Knight (2008)
 This movie set the bar for what comic book movies could be as a serious drama. Heath Ledger gives an Oscar winning performance as the Joker and the large amount of IMAX footage gives the movie an epic feeling. 9.0/10
Bill & Ted's Bogus Journey (1991)
 A wacky sequel to the most bodacious Excellent Adventure. I would say the original is objectively a better movie, but I find this one more enjoyable due to how ridiculous it is and the charming practical effects. Also, William Sadler is hilarious as death. 6.3/10
Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back (1980)
 One of the most famous movies of all time. Empire Strikes back boasts an epic score, innovative special effects, a classic twist, and an ending where it seems the villains win. 8.7/10
Blade Runner 2049 (2017)
 Denis Villeneuve established himself as one of my favourite directors after directing this movie and 2016's Arrival. Blade Runner 2049 delivers jaw-dropping visuals and a story that both honors the original and delivers an original story. Even though this movie is almost an hour longer than the original I think the pacing is better and makes 2049 a more enjoyable watch. 8.7/10
